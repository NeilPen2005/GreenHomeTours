Sherry Listgarten's Home

4BR 2BA Eichler ranch style home

built in 1955. Remodeled 2010.

Electric Service: 200 amp main panel; 100 sub

Highlights

Electric Radiant Heating and Cooling

Efficient 3-Ton Heat Pump

Ducted Heat Pump Water Heater

Foam Roof and Extensive Insulation

100% LED Lighting

Low-Water Landscaping

Appliances and Green Features

Heating Ventilation & Air Conditioning: Radiant Floor with 3 Zones (heated and cooled), Air-to-Water Heat Pump (Chiltrix), Buffer Tank for Radiant Floor (Chiltrix, Fan Coils for heating and cooling (Jaga)

Water Heater: Heat Pump (Rheem)

Smart Thermostat: (Nest)

Insulation: Durafoam foam roof, Exterior wall insulation

Windows: Dual-pane, Window shades

Lighting: LED lights

Chimney Plug

Landscape: Low-water plants, Mulched lawn area, Largely drip irrigation, 7-day and 10-day irrigation cycles

EV Charger: JuiceBox
