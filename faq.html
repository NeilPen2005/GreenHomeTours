FAQ

When is the event?

September 24, 2022 from 9am-3pm.

Where is the event?

We are hosted all around the Bay Area. Our three hotspots include San Mateo, Palo Alto/Menlo Park, and San Jose/Sunnyvale/Cupertino. Under Tour Info, check the Green Homes tab to see which Green Homes you can tour.

What features will there be?

We have electric vehicles, induction stoves and cooking demos, energy-efficient home appliances, and more! Check out the Green Homes tab for details about each home.

Can I go to multiple homes?

Yes! Feel free to visit as many as you like.

Do I have to sign up?

Yes; it helps us make sure we can accommodate all guests. Please go to the Register tab to sign up.

I cannot make the event but want to learn more. What should I do?

We will be hosting this event next year as well. You can also check out our Resources tab for easy green swaps and ideas to be more sustainable.
