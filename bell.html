Appliances and Green Features

HVAC: Electric Heat Pump Furnace & Air Conditioner (MrCool mini split heat pump space heater (heat and air conditioning using the equivalent of two hair dryers of electricity) ; cassette upstairs), cassette upstairs

Kitchen: Induction cooktop and Electric oven (fast heating, easy temp control, cooler kitchen, no gas fumes)

Water Heating: Booster electric water heater (bathroom) delivers hot water in 10 seconds to shower

Laundry: Wiring for heat pump clothes dryer (future); Greywater feeds landscape (20% of landscape use)

Lighting: LED bulbs, some fluorescent

Skylights: Ventilated and tinted (polycarbonate)

Indoor Air Quality Thermostat- circulates once/min in summer

Insulation: added to exterior walls, Garage ceiling; Thermo bath floor covering

Roof ridge vent and attic fans, Ceiling exhaust fan for heat dissipation

Fireplace: opening closed semi-perm

Solar panels (18 currently)

Pomona tile re-existing

Electric Vehicles: VW ID4 and Honda Clarity plug-in hybrid

EV charger: 240 volt EV charger, designed for low amp install with small 100 amp electric panel, allows for later wiring upgrade

Landscape: Weather enabled sprinkler controller, Electric gardening tools (EGO)

Cottage features under-sink hot water demand pump)
