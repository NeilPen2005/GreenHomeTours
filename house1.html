Design & Appliances

Air Admittance Valves (AAVs)

Durable, one-way air valve, the size of a large vitamin bottle. Reduces need for membrane punctures and plumbing piping.

White Metal Roof

Light colored roof reflects more solar heat, decreasing the temperature inside of the building, which reduces energy use associated with cooling

Daikin Altherma heat pump

Creates 3-4 times more heat from the same electricity as a standard electric water heater

Radiant Floor Heating

Radiant slabs heat using lukewarm water (90ºF), compared to other heating equipment such as radiators and baseboards (160ºF).

Electric Lighting

Lighting is either Light Emitting Diode (LED) or fluorescent. LED lights and compact fluorescents save significantly over incandescent bulbs.

Concrete Slab Flooring

Made with materials often considered waste products, which require less energy to process.

Eco Flush Toilets

The toilet has two compartments (solids and urine) that flush seperately, urine flushes use as little as 0.04 gallons (7 ounces). Old style toilets use 200 ounces and modern dual-flush use 100-153 ounces

Rainwater Collection

Rainwater from all the roofs is collected and channeled to the northeastern side of the house (back right). At present it runs via a “rock stream” into a dry well

Induction Stove

With an inductive stove, 84% of the energy goes to heat the food versus 75% for a typical electrical stove and only 40% for a gas stove.
