Appliances and Green Features

Heating Ventilation and Air Conditioning: (Ducted Heat Pumps (Carrier), with thermostats (EcoBee) & MERV 13 filters, ducts and registers sealed and insulated Ductless Heat Pump for ADU)

Water Heater: Split System Heat Pump Water Heater (SanC02) - outdoor heat pump, indoor storage tank Heat Pump Water Heater (Bradford White)

Kitchen: Induction Cooktop (Wolf) 5-hob Electric Ovens

Laundry: Hybrid Heat Pump Dryer (LG )

Solar Panels: 7.84 kWdc SolarWorld Sunmodule panels, SolarEdge inverter

Electric Vehicle: 2020 (Nissan Leaf)

EV Charger: Two EV Chargers on one circuit, utilizing automatic load management system (JuiceBox Enel X)

Insulation: Enhanced air sealing and crawl space insulation

Windows: replaced single-pane with dual pane
